My Background

I was born in the bourbon capital of Louisville, Kentucky. Today, I'm in the dairy homeland of Wisconsin with hopes of moving to Chicago, Illinois.

On weekends you'll find me watching any sport, playing online party games with friends, diving deep down a tech rabbit hole or trying out new recipes. Being from Louisville, college basketball is one of my favorite things to watch, closely followed by college football and the NFL. You should know I'm a BIG Lousville Cardinals fan, an avid Lamar Jackson fan and a Cheesehead.

On weekdays, you'll catch me marathon training, practicing French on Duolingo, developing personal projects and cheffing up something absolutely delicious in the kitchen. I deeply value the physical and mental health benefits of long distance running, it also helps that i'm not too shabby at it. Typically, i'll be listening to 70s, 80s or any upbeat music that makes you just wanna bust a move, boogie down or plain ol' dance around. Nothin beats a classic foot tapper and hip shaker.

Professionally speaking, I have 3 core tenets to my personality: Curiosity, Discipline, and Continuous Improvement. Individually, each tenet feeds into the other. When combined, the ability to learn, adapt and grow is not only expected, but ingrained in my day to day. Finding working solutions is nice, however, I consistently strive to find optimal solutions accounting for optimization, management, and future integrations or enhancements.

My broad technical skillset allows me to be versatile while my life skills allow me to develop and improve solutions that not only work for, but enhance, the lives of those around me.
